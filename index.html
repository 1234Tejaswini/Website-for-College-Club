About Competitive Exam Cell

Competitive Exam Cell, shortly abbreviated as CEC, is a club which comes under the Department of Students Affair at JSPM’S RSCOE. The main motive of the club is to spread awareness about job opportunities in Public Sectors, enhance ‘Officer-like Qualities’ (OLQs), and develop communication skills by organizing events like debates, essay writing, etc.

  • The main aim of the club is to provide opportunities for students to broaden their knowledge within governmental, defence, UPSC examinations.
  • CEC will also expand students' leadership skills by conducting lectures with various educators, government officers, and aspirants.
  • The club was started as a solution to the problem that "Engineering Students lack in preparation for Competitive Exams." Here, the aspirants can prepare for their exams with like-minded students, fostering a productive environment to excel in competitive exams.
